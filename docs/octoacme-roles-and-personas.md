# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Responsible for user experience research, prototypes, and interaction design.

### Responsibilities
- Develop wireframes, prototypes, and interaction flows
- Collaborate with Product Manager and Developers on feasibility and design trade-offs
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Advocate for user-centric decisions in planning and delivery

### Goals
Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences; validate designs with real user feedback; ensure consistency across product surfaces.

### Typical Communication
Design reviews, sprint planning, stakeholder presentations, collaboration with Product Managers on requirements.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Partners with Product Managers to validate requirements; reviews implementations with Developers and QA; participates in stakeholder reviews.

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Manages CI/CD pipelines, release automation, and infrastructure reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Configure and maintain build, test, and deploy pipelines
- Monitor environment health, performance, and security
- Coordinate releases and rollback plans
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and scaling

### Goals
Ensure reliable, repeatable deployments; minimize downtime; improve developer productivity through automation.

### Typical Communication
Release coordination meetings, incident response channels, infrastructure reviews, CI/CD status dashboards.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Works with Developers for code deployment; coordinates with QA for test environment configuration; supports Project Managers in release/post-release activities.

---

## QA / Testing Engineer

### Role Summary
Validates product quality against acceptance criteria through manual and automated testing.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans (manual and automated)
- Validate acceptance criteria before releases
- Report and track defects
- Maintain automated test suites and regression tests
- Participate in release readiness reviews

### Goals
Ensure releases meet quality standards; catch regressions early; maintain comprehensive test coverage.

### Typical Communication
Sprint planning, defect triage meetings, release readiness reviews, test status reports.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Works with Developers to reproduce and fix defects; validates Product Manager acceptance criteria; coordinates with DevOps on test environments; reports quality status to Project Managers.

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Provides strategic direction, funding authorization, and high-level approval for projects.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project charters and major scope changes
- Provide business context and strategic priorities
- Participate in go/no-go decisions
- Remove organizational blockers when escalated

### Goals
Ensure projects align with business strategy; maximize ROI; maintain visibility into project health.

### Typical Communication
Monthly stakeholder updates, milestone reviews, escalation meetings, go/no-go decisions.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Receives status reports from Project Managers; aligns on priorities with Product Managers; final escalation point for blockers.

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Provides data-driven insights to inform product decisions, measure success metrics, and track project health.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics and KPIs
- Analyze user behavior and product performance data
- Build dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Support retrospectives with quantitative insights

### Goals
Enable data-informed decision-making; surface actionable insights; track impact of shipped features.

### Typical Communication
Sprint reviews, stakeholder briefings, dashboard updates, retrospective data presentations.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Partners with Product Managers on metrics definition; supports Project Managers with project health data; collaborates with Developers on instrumentation and logging.

---

## Documentation Lead

### Role Summary
Owns the creation, maintenance, and quality of project and process documentation.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain process docs, runbooks, and onboarding materials
- Ensure documentation stays current with process changes
- Review and edit contributions from other team members
- Establish documentation standards and templates

### Goals
Keep institutional knowledge accessible and up-to-date; reduce onboarding time; ensure consistency across all documentation.

### Typical Communication
Documentation reviews, sprint planning (for doc tasks), retrospective follow-ups, style guide updates.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Coordinates with Project Managers on process doc updates; works with Developers on technical documentation; partners with Product Managers on user-facing content.

---

## Support Engineer

### Role Summary
Acts as the bridge between customers and the engineering team, triaging issues and escalating bugs.

### Responsibilities
- Triage incoming support tickets and customer-reported issues
- Reproduce and document bugs for the development team
- Communicate workarounds and resolutions to customers
- Provide feedback to Product Managers on common pain points

### Goals
Resolve customer issues quickly; reduce mean time to resolution; surface recurring problems for product improvements.

### Typical Communication
Support ticket queues, incident channels, weekly bug triage meetings, customer feedback summaries.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
Escalates bugs to Developers; provides customer context to Product Managers; coordinates with Project Managers on incident timelines; works with QA on defect verification.

---

- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- This expanded set of personas covers the full cross-functional team, ensuring clear ownership and collaboration patterns across all project activities.
